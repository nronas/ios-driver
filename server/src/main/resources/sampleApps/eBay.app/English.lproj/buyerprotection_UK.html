How does eBay buyer protection work?

1. Contact the seller

If your item hasn't arrived or isn't as described, contact the seller first. Give them a chance to make things right for you. You can contact your seller by following these steps
  • Tap Home at the bottom of the screen
  • Tap Buying at the top of the screen
  • In the Won section, select the item you want to contact the seller about
  • From the listing, scroll down and tap Contact Seller

2. Still not resolved? Get assistance from customer service

Our customer support specialists are here to help. Just contact us if your item hasn't arrived or isn't as described to open a case in the Resolution Centre following the steps below.
  • Go to the eBay website using your browser or computer
  • At the top of the homepage, hover over Help, and then click on Resolution Centre
If you have any issues with opening the case or any questions regarding the process our customer service specialist are here to assist you. We're available for questions on 0800 358 6551 from 8:00 AM-10:00 PM Monday to Friday and 9:00 AM -6:00 PM at weekends.

3. We'll work with the seller to resolve the issue

We'll contact the seller and ask them to promptly resolve the issue. After 8 days, we'll ask you how it's going with the seller. If you tell us that the problem cannot be resolved between you and the seller, and your case qualifies for and is covered by eBay Buyer Protection, eBay will refund your full purchase price plus original shipping into your PayPal account.
