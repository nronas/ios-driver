To ensure that you will receive eBay Mobile push notifications:
- The item must be added to your Watch List even if you have bid on the item
- "Keep me logged in" must be set to ON in eBay Mobile settings
- Notifications must be turned ON in the device System Settings
- The wifi network you are on must support push notification traffic

Push notifications will not work if:
- You are using a Jail Broken device
- Your iPod Touch device is in the sleep mode
